Editorial

Delays?

This issue of Scorpio News is probably reaching you much later than many of you expected. To be fair to us, we have not yet published the 1988 issue dates, but to be fair to you, Issue 1 last year was about a month earlier.

Why the delay? Well, last year we discovered that deciding to send an issue out at the end of December was a big mistake. Firstly, printers are exceedingly busy in the run up to Christmas, and secondly it isn’t much fun packing up and sending out newsletters when you know that everyone else is stuffed full of turkey and booze and has their feet up watching the ninety-third re-run of “The Wizard of Oz”!
